Eggplant Parmesan Recipe

Description: Indulge in the rich and savory flavors of Eggplant Parmesan!

Ingredients

  • Eggplants, sliced
  • Italian breadcrumbs
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe Steps

  • Step 1: Preheat the oven to 375Â°F.
  • Step 2: Dip eggplant slices in beaten eggs, then coat with breadcrumbs.
  • Step 3: Arrange the slices in a baking dish, layer with tomato sauce, cheeses, and basil.
  • Step 4: Repeat the layers and finish with a layer of cheese on top.
  • Step 5: Bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly, about 30 minutes.
  • Step 6: Let it cool slightly before serving.
