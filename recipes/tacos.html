Tasty Taco Recipe

Description: Savor the flavor of these delicious homemade tacos!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef or shredded chicken
  • Taco shells
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Shredded cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa
  • Guacamole

Recipe Steps

  • Step 1: Cook the ground beef or shred the chicken.
  • Step 2: Warm the taco shells in the oven.
  • Step 3: Assemble the tacos with your favorite toppings.
  • Step 4: Enjoy your tasty homemade tacos!
